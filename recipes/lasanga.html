Best Ever Meat Loaf

Prep Time:
20 mins
Cook Time:
1 hr
Total Time:
1 hr 20 mins
Servings:
6
Why you'll love this recipe
  • This meat loaf recipe features baked-in Cheddar cheese for rich flavor and a gooey texture.
  • Home cooks say the savory-sweet glaze creates a juicy loaf that wins over even picky eaters.
  • Allrecipes member Donna claims, "This was absolutely the best meatloaf I have ever made."

Ingredients

  • 2 large egss
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 slice bread, crumbled
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 shredded carrot

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and ground black pepper in a large bowl. Add crumbled bread and stir until dissolved. Mix ground beef, onion, Cheddar cheese, and carrot into bread mixture; transfer mixture to a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard in a small bowl; spread over the meat mixture.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 60 to 75 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
